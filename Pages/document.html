Document Attestation

Kashi Overseas Man Power Pvt. Ltd. is leading multinational recruitment company sharing a trusted partnership with Ministry of External Affairs (MEA), Government of India and Consulate/ Embassies to carry out all the administrative process of document attestation / Apostille. We handle all the governmental and non-governmental tasks related to recruitment services, visa stamping and certificate attestation to serve the interest of our client’s government thus, facilitating them to offer their prime focus towards the assessment of the candidates.

Kashi Overseas Man Power Pvt. Ltd. recognises your issue and provides the best meaapostille services. We have established solid relationships with several government agencies and officials that help us make the procedure simpler and less time-consuming thanks to our collective efforts and the help of our professionals. We have improved our grasp of this process over the years, so we can guarantee that we will get your documents attested in a remarkably short amount of time.

We are a pioneer in this industry thanks to our extensive expertise and knowledge, which gives us the advantage of supplying comprehensive attestation services in a quick and simple way. We accept papers from both persons and representatives and provide a range of visa stamping from the Ministry of HRD, the Ministry of External Affairs, embassies, consulates, and other relevant government agencies. Similar to this, NRIs (Non-Resident Indians) who need assistance obtaining their state document attestation attested from any State HRD, Notary, MEA, or any Embassy in India can get in touch with our organisation for first-rate support.
