Kashi Overseas Man Power Pvt. Ltd.

Established in the year 2005, Kashi Overseas Man Power Pvt. Ltd. is owned by HR Group of Companies and is known to be an accredited global company situated in the hustling & bustling capital city New Delhi, India. The company has a diverse conglomerate holdings in manufacturing, oil & gas, information technology, outsourcing, recruitment, healthcare, business setup, immigration etc. HR International has developed a solid reputation and a niche expertise in the human resource as well as other sectors over the past 19 years. Considered to be one of the biggest human resource companies in India, our company has broad geographic reach and comprehensive knowledge of finding the best talent strength from all across the globe.

Kashi Overseas Man Power Pvt. Ltd. contributes to finding the best talent from across the nation. We deploy candidates to numerous nations such as Saudi Arabia, UAE, Kuwait, Europe, and UK from various backgrounds of healthcare, engineering, hospitality, information technology, oil & gas and many more. It has effectively attained the status of a leading domestic/international recruitment company and established a standard among its clients and business associates. Additionally, it has received approval from the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA), whose head office is in the prestigious Indian city of New Delhi and which also has a branch office in Mumbai & Cochin and global offices in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia & UAE.

Additionally, it has received approval from the Ministry of Higher Education (MOHE) Kingdom of Saudi Arabia to facilitate the recruitment of qualified Indians for higher faculty positions and to give them a platform to demonstrate their value in the academic sector. Both organizations are recognized and approved by the Ministry of Health (MOH) Kingdom of Saudi Arabia for the recruitment of Medical professionals and other medical cadres in Saudi Government Hospitals and Healthcare Organizations. HRI strongly believes in its client’s satisfaction and facilitation, thus H.R Technical Trade Centre Pvt. Ltd. is another extension of the organization, accredited by National Skill Development Corporation (NSDC), Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY), Centre for Skill Development of Construction Industries (CSDCI) and certain other noble organizations, viz. Quality Council of India- NABET, which imparts technical skill knowledge to the candidates and makes them upgraded with the advanced industrial equipments.
