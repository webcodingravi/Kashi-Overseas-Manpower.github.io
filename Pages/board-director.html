Board of Directors

Together, we laid the foundation for H.R. International about 18 years ago, and it has since grown into a strong, innovative, and prosperous business entity. We learned that we had been successful in every commercial effort we had previously undertaken. We were able to maintain the integrity and variety of our business since we began by providing top-notch service to all of our domestic and international customers.
The importance of a company's relevance to its partners, customers, and other stakeholders is something that we as board of directors frequently discuss passionately. We are the creators of the business, and we have extensive experience working with charitable groups, higher education, and community development. In a relatively short period of time, we as a corporation have already observed a flourishing reputation across our multiple business units.
Due to the wide range of services we offer in several business sectors, we were able to garner numerous honours and accolades from various business groups. All the well-known business sectors, including Real Estate, Tours and Travel, IT, Diagnostics & Healthcare are already adding incredible success stories to the group of Companies.
We feel proud to inform you that our business has been appreciated which made us capable of winning various awards. The appreciations and awards, we get entitled for, encourage us and this encouragement enables us to keep the continuous growth speeding up. We have been bestowed with award of “Best Performer” for our honest and quality services. We feel highly grateful to announce that H.R. Tour & Travel serves pilgrimage touring needs by planning trouble free religious trip of HAJJ and UMRAH. Besides, we feel proud to recall that our H.R. Tours & Travels had been accredited by IATA (International Air Transport Associations) which has enabled us to channelize traveling for our customers in much smooth verses.
We feel happy to announce that recently we opened a diagnostic and healthcare unit, i.e. H.R. Diagnostic as a sister concern of H.R. International in order to serve the community and the people around us with the best ever services and also as an asset to H.R. international for undergoing various medical checkups for the selected candidates at various posts in different companies round the Globe.
Our most appreciating and efficient management is well complimented for understanding the ends and outs of running a business by bearing in mind growing needs of complex organizations. All this is done successfully to ensure our rapid growth as a strong multitude business group.
With continuous efforts and business strategies which were laid by H.R. Group almost 18 years ago seems to earn innumerable and consistent laurels in all the widespread business sectors. We would not forget to thank all our esteemed domestic and overseas clients who contributed to the success of our company and becoming one of the most reputed service providers in the global market.
We would like to extend accolades the well equipped staff of HR Group who sincerely gratify their sources and hardships to make these years, an immensely successful business journey. At the end, we all would like to again sincerely thank all our associates in believing us and cooperating with us to achieve success of the group. These efforts of working hard day and night have been a noteworthy dedication for the organization.
