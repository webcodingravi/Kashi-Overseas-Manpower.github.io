Healthcare

In the healthcare sector, Kashi Overseas Man Power Pvt. Ltd. is a well-known multinational recruitment company that has developed a reputation for finding the best candidates. Under the direction of the MOH (Ministry of Health), the Government of Saudi Arabia, we are an authorised nurse staffing agency for the recruitment of health care professionals, including consultants, specialists, residents, and general practitioners, as well as other professional nursing (B.Sc) & paramedical staff, for government hospitals across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.
We are specialized in healthcare recruitment with over 3000+ deployment of nurses to renowned hospitals like Al Moosa, Al Mouwasat, Al Salam, Al Dara Specialist hospitals. We cater to healthcare staffing from Asia, Europe, Canada etc. and proffer 100% satisfaction rate. Moreover we deploy both B.Sc and G.Nm nurses who are looking to work and settle overseas. We are known for our fast and pro-active services using our global team who work around the clock ensuring quality and top notch service.
We have helped a significant number of patient care jobs in the healthcare sector, and we are regarded as one of the most popular Best healthcare agencies in India, Middle East & European countries, particularly for senior level medical professionals. Furthermore, we have built a solid reputation for providing standard employment aid in the healthcare industry sectors since we immediately and expertly handle any demands made by our reputable international clientele.
We are able to find highly qualified and experienced registered medical and paramedical professionals for the renowned international healthcare institutes and hospitals thanks to our team of experienced professional head, Team Lead, and recruiters for the medical field careers. They conduct their search in accordance with their understanding of the industry's working conditions. These individuals have extensive understanding of the health sector and may actively contribute to its improvement. We additionally examine the applicants' employment history to confirm their professional background.
HR International is one of the fastest growing healthcare service provider in Saudi Arabia. We take great satisfaction in offering our clients and applicants the best possible individualized service. We actually have access to the best personnel and opportunities in the healthcare sector and offer excellent and hassle free service across the globe.
Our main objective at HR International is to find possibilities for registered doctors and nurses with relevant experience and skill sets and assist them in landing well-paying jobs. These jobs are available in many different hospitals and healthcare facilities across the globe.
We also take great pride in being the top healthcare service provider in UAE, providing the greatest doctors and nurses available. In order to help healthcare firms find the most qualified applicants for open positions, we accomplish this by adhering to a rigid and organized recruitment process. It is always our duty as best healthcare agencies in India to make sure that candidates have sufficient first-hand experience and documentation of their professional background.

We specialize in the recruitment of Health care professionals, such as Consultant, Specialist, Resident, & G.P, along with Nursing (B.Sc) & Paramedical staff, covering the following disciplines-
